About This Module

The Weekly Intelligence Dashboard delivers automated cybersecurity threat intelligence reports focused on the LATAM and Spain regions. Our system analyzes regional threats, vulnerabilities, and incidents to provide actionable insights for security teams.

This module is one of three core components in the Laberit Intelligence platform, working in conjunction with the DII 4.0 Framework and Quick Assessment tools to deliver comprehensive security intelligence.
