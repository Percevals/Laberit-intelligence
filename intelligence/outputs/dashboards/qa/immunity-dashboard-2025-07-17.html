Major Mexican Bank (undisclosed) - Financial

High

Municipal Government System - Government

📍 Colombia 📅 2025-07-15 🏢 SERVICIOS DATOS
Critical

💡 Lección ejecutiva: Government entities must prioritize backup modernization and testing to maintain citizen services
